Welcome to Bicknell’s Barkers

At Bicknell’s Barkers, we offer trusted doggy daycare and home boarding in a safe, loving, family environment. Whether it’s a day of fun or a longer stay, your dog will enjoy walks on private land, a cosy dog room, and plenty of cuddles.

About Us

Welcome to Bicknell’s Barkers – your trusted provider of doggy daycare and home boarding in north Fareham and the surrounding areas.

We are a family-run business passionate about providing a safe, stimulating, and loving environment for your four-legged friends. Whether you’re heading to work or away on holiday, you can rest assured that your dog is in caring and capable hands.

We are:

  • Fully insured
  • 5-star licensed by Fareham Borough Council
  • Level 3 qualified in canine first aid
  • Trained in dog behaviour and handling

At Bicknell’s Barkers, your dog will enjoy:

  • A dedicated dog room designed for comfort and safety
  • Walks on private land
  • A loving, home-from-home setting where they are treated as part of the family

We understand that every dog is unique, and we take pride in offering personalised care tailored to each dog’s needs. Whether your dog loves playtime, cuddles, or quiet naps, we’re here to make sure they feel right at home.

We can’t wait to welcome your furry friend into the Bicknell’s Barkers family!
